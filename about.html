What is WhichCollege?

WhichCollege is a platform where students who are shortlisting and applying for universities are matched with mentors who are currently studying in the university where the student is applying. At the same time getting access to curcial information about colleges, courses, exams etc.

Why WhichCollege?

Whether it is a student who has just taken his class 10th board exams and wants to know the best coaching for JEE or an experienced executive looking to take his career to next level with an executive MBA. Students of all ages face a lot of confusion while searching and deciding the right place for their education. According to a National Student Survey, 1 in 5 students are unhappy with their university course. Many students in colleges are unsatisfied with their course or the college, this realisation comes at a great cost, both financially and time-wise. Had they been able to speak to students attending the universities, to hear their invaluable information about their courses, campuses, and city lives, they would certainly have made more informed and better decisions.
